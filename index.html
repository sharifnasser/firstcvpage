Some hobbies

Sharif is a 4th-year Digital System and Robotics student at Tecnológico de Monterrey in México. During his undergraduate studies, he has participated in several extracurricular activities that allowed him to lead student groups, volunteer as an instructor for young people, become a student ambassador, and work as an Industry 4.0 Intern.

Driven by his passion for technology and education, Sharif joined the Microsoft Student Partners (now Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors) program last year. Since then, he takes pride in teaching and amplifying his impact on hundreds of young and adult Latin Americans who have attended his events. Currently, he also serves as a Peer Mentor for first-year students and dedicates his free time to learn about AI and Deep Learning.

As a soon graduate, his goals include joining a company that seeks to shape a better world for everyone, pursuing a master's degree involving research experience, and empowering people - probably not in that order.
